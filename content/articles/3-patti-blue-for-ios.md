## Is 3 Patti Blue Available on iPhone?

3 Patti Blue does not have an official App Store listing. However, iOS users can still play using the **mobile web browser version** or by using **AltStore** to sideload the app. Here are all available methods.

## Method 1 — Play via Mobile Browser (Recommended for iOS)

The easiest way for iPhone/iPad users:

## Step 1 — Open Safari

Open Safari on your iPhone or iPad and visit the official 3 Patti Blue website.

## Step 2 — Log In or Register

[Sign up for an account](/3-patti-blue-signup) or [log in](/3-patti-blue-login) if you already have one. The mobile web version works smoothly on iOS 14 and above.

## Step 3 — Add to Home Screen

Tap the **Share** button in Safari and select **Add to Home Screen**. This creates a shortcut that feels like a native app.

## Method 2 — AltStore Sideloading

For users who want the full APK experience on iPhone:

## Step 1 — Install AltStore

Download AltStore from [altstore.io](https://altstore.io) on your Mac or Windows PC. Install AltServer on your computer.

## Step 2 — Connect Your iPhone

Connect your iPhone via USB. Open AltServer and install AltStore onto your iPhone.

## Step 3 — Download the IPA File

Download the 3 Patti Blue IPA file. Open AltStore on your iPhone, tap the **+** button, and select the IPA file.

## Step 4 — Trust the App

Go to **Settings → General → VPN & Device Management** and trust the developer certificate. Now open 3 Patti Blue.

## iOS Requirements

- iPhone with iOS 13 or higher
- iPad with iPadOS 13 or higher
- Stable internet connection (Wi-Fi or 4G/5G)

- Use Safari for the best web version experience
- Enable notifications to receive bonus alerts
