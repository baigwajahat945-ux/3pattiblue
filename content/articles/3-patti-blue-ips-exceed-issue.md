## What is the "IPs Exceed" Error in 3 Patti Blue?

Many 3 Patti Blue players in Pakistan have reported seeing an **"IPs Exceed"** error message when trying to log in or play. This error means that too many devices or IP addresses have tried to access the same account within a short period of time. The system detects this as suspicious activity and temporarily blocks access to protect your account.

Do not panic — this issue is very common and can be fixed in minutes by following the steps below.

## Why Does the IPs Exceed Error Happen?

There are several reasons this error can appear:

- **Multiple devices logged in** — you are using the same account on more than one phone, tablet, or PC at the same time
- **VPN or proxy usage** — switching VPNs or using a proxy changes your IP address frequently, which triggers the limit
- **Shared Wi-Fi network** — if multiple players in the same house or office are using one Wi-Fi connection, the server sees multiple logins from the same IP
- **App logged in on emulator and phone simultaneously** — running 3 Patti Blue on BlueStacks and your phone at the same time counts as two separate IPs
- **Account shared with others** — sharing your login credentials with friends or family causes multiple IP conflicts
- **Unstable internet** — frequent disconnections and reconnections can sometimes assign you a new IP each time, triggering the limit

## How to Fix the IPs Exceed Issue in 3 Patti Blue

## Fix 1 — Log Out of All Devices

The quickest fix is to log out from all devices and log back in from a single device only.

1. Open 3 Patti Blue on your main phone
2. Go to **Settings → Account → Log Out of All Devices**
3. Wait 5 minutes
4. Log back in on your phone only

## Fix 2 — Turn Off Your VPN

If you are using a VPN, turn it off completely before opening 3 Patti Blue.

1. Close 3 Patti Blue
2. Disable your VPN from your phone's settings or VPN app
3. Wait 2 minutes for your IP to stabilise
4. Re-open 3 Patti Blue and log in normally

## Fix 3 — Switch from Wi-Fi to Mobile Data

If you are on a shared Wi-Fi network, try switching to your mobile data connection instead.

1. Turn off Wi-Fi on your phone
2. Enable mobile data (Jazz, Zong, Telenor, Ufone)
3. Open 3 Patti Blue and log in again

## Fix 4 — Clear App Cache

Sometimes outdated cached session data causes IP conflicts.

1. Go to **Settings → Apps → 3 Patti Blue**
2. Tap **Storage → Clear Cache**
3. Do NOT tap Clear Data (this will remove your login)
4. Reopen the app and log in

## Fix 5 — Reinstall the Latest APK

If the above fixes do not work, uninstall and reinstall the latest version of 3 Patti Blue.

1. Uninstall 3 Patti Blue from your phone
2. [Download the latest APK](/) from our site
3. Install it fresh and log in with your credentials

## Fix 6 — Contact 3 Patti Blue Support

If none of the above fixes work within 24 hours, contact 3 Patti Blue customer support:

- Open the app and tap the **Live Chat** icon
- Explain the "IPs Exceed" error and provide your registered phone number
- Support will manually reset your IP limit within a few hours

## How to Prevent the IPs Exceed Error in Future

> **Use only one device at a time.** Never share your login credentials with anyone. Avoid VPNs while playing 3 Patti Blue. Always use a stable internet connection.

- Never log in on multiple devices simultaneously
- Do not share your account with friends or family — each person should [create their own account](/3-patti-blue-signup)
- Avoid switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data repeatedly while the app is open
- Keep the app updated to the latest version at all times
- Do not use VPN apps while playing

## Summary

The IPs Exceed error is a security feature of 3 Patti Blue that activates when too many IP addresses access one account. The fastest fixes are turning off your VPN, logging out of all devices, or switching to mobile data. If the problem persists, reinstall the app or contact support directly. With these steps you will be back playing in no time.
