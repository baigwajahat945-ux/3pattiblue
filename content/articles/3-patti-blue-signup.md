## How to Create a 3 Patti Blue Account

Creating your 3 Patti Blue account is completely free and takes under 2 minutes. Here is how:

## Step 1 — Download and Install

First, [download the 3 Patti Blue APK](/) and install it on your Android phone.

## Step 2 — Open the App and Tap Sign Up

Launch the app. On the welcome screen, tap the **Sign Up** or **Register** button.

## Step 3 — Enter Your Phone Number

Type your Pakistani mobile number (starting with 03). This will be your account username.

## Step 4 — Verify via OTP

You will receive a 6-digit OTP code via SMS. Enter it in the verification field. The code expires in 60 seconds.

## Step 5 — Create a Password

Choose a strong password with at least 8 characters, mixing letters and numbers.

## Step 6 — Enter Referral Code (Optional)

If a friend referred you, enter their referral code to get a bonus for both of you.

## Step 7 — Claim Your Welcome Bonus

After registration, go to the **Promotions** or **Bonus** section and claim your welcome bonus. New players get up to PKR 5,000 on their first deposit.

## Registration Requirements

- You must be **18 years or older**
- A valid Pakistani mobile number (Jazz, Zong, Telenor, Ufone, Warid)
- One account per person is allowed

## Tips After Sign Up

- **Complete your profile** — add your name and verify your account for faster withdrawals
- **Enable notifications** — never miss a bonus or promotion
- **Add a payment method** — link JazzCash or EasyPaisa from the Cashier section
- **Read the bonus terms** — understand wagering requirements before claiming bonuses
