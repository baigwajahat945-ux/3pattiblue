## What is the 3 Patti Blue VIP Program?

The **3 Patti Blue VIP Program** is a loyalty system that rewards regular players with exclusive benefits the more they play. As you wager and deposit, you accumulate VIP points that unlock higher tiers with better perks — from faster withdrawals to personal account managers and higher betting limits.

The VIP Program is free to join. Every player starts at Bronze level and can work their way up to Diamond and beyond.

## How to Earn VIP Points

Every PKR you wager earns you VIP points. The conversion rate depends on the game type:

- **Teen Patti & Card Games:** 1 VIP point per PKR 10 wagered
- **Slots & Fishing Games:** 1 VIP point per PKR 5 wagered
- **Sports Betting:** 1 VIP point per PKR 20 wagered
- **Aviator / Crash Games:** 1 VIP point per PKR 15 wagered
