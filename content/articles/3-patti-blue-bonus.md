## All 3 Patti Blue Bonuses Explained

3 Patti Blue offers one of the most generous bonus programs among Pakistani gaming apps. Here is a full breakdown of every offer available.

## Welcome Bonus

**New players** receive a deposit bonus on their first top-up:

- Deposit PKR 500 → Get PKR 500 bonus (100% match)
- Deposit PKR 1,000 → Get PKR 1,000 bonus
- Maximum welcome bonus: **PKR 5,000**

To claim: [Sign up](/3-patti-blue-signup), make your first deposit, and the bonus is credited automatically.

## Daily Login Bonus

Log in every day to collect free chips and spin rewards:

- **Day 1:** PKR 20 free chips
- **Day 3:** PKR 50 free chips + 1 free spin
- **Day 7:** PKR 100 free chips + 3 free spins
- **Day 30:** PKR 500 free chips + special prize

## Referral Bonus

Invite friends and earn for every person who joins:

- **You get:** PKR 200 for each friend who registers and deposits
- **Your friend gets:** Extra PKR 100 welcome bonus
- **How to refer:** Go to **Me → Referral** in the app and share your unique link

## Weekly Cashback

If you have a losing week, 3 Patti Blue gives back 10% of your net losses:

- Cashback is calculated every Monday
- Minimum loss to qualify: PKR 500
- Cashback is credited as bonus chips (not real money)

## Special Event Bonuses

During Pakistani holidays (Eid, Independence Day), 3 Patti Blue runs limited-time offers:

- Double cashback weekends
- Lucky draw with cash prizes
- Free spin events

## How to Claim Bonuses

## Step 1 — Go to Promotions

Open the app and tap **Promotions** or **Bonus** from the bottom menu.

## Step 2 — Select Your Bonus

Tap on the bonus you want to claim and read the terms carefully.

## Step 3 — Meet the Requirements

Some bonuses require a minimum deposit or meeting wagering requirements before withdrawal.

## Step 4 — Enjoy

The bonus amount is added to your account immediately after claiming.

## Bonus Terms to Know

- **Wagering requirement:** Most bonuses require 5x–10x playthrough before withdrawal
- **Expiry:** Bonuses expire after 7–30 days if not used
- **Game restrictions:** Some bonuses only work on specific games
