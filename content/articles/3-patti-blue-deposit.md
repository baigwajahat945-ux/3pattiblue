## How to Deposit Money in 3 Patti Blue

Adding money to your 3 Patti Blue account is quick and easy. The app supports Pakistan's most popular payment methods — JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and bank transfer — with deposits credited instantly.

## Deposit Methods

| Method | Min Amount | Max Amount | Processing Time |
|--------|-----------|------------|-----------------|
| JazzCash | PKR 200 | PKR 50,000 | Instant |
| EasyPaisa | PKR 200 | PKR 50,000 | Instant |
| Bank Transfer | PKR 500 | PKR 200,000 | 1–3 hours |

## Step-by-Step Deposit Guide

## Step 1 — Log In to Your Account

Open 3 Patti Blue and [log in to your account](/3-patti-blue-login). If you don't have an account yet, [sign up first](/3-patti-blue-signup) — it takes under 2 minutes.

## Step 2 — Go to the Wallet / Cashier

Tap the **Wallet** or **Cashier** icon at the bottom of the app screen.

## Step 3 — Tap Deposit

Select **Deposit** from the available options. You will see a list of payment methods.

## Step 4 — Choose Your Payment Method

Select **JazzCash** or **EasyPaisa** for the fastest processing. Both are instant and widely available across Pakistan.

## Step 5 — Enter the Amount

Type the amount you want to deposit. The minimum deposit is **PKR 200**.

## Step 6 — Enter Your Mobile Number

Enter your JazzCash or EasyPaisa registered mobile number. Make sure the number is correct before proceeding.

## Step 7 — Confirm and Pay

Tap **Confirm**. You will receive an OTP on your mobile number. Enter the OTP to authorise the payment.

## Step 8 — Funds Added

Your deposit will be credited to your 3 Patti Blue wallet instantly. You will see your updated balance on the home screen.

## How to Deposit via Bank Transfer

## Step 1 — Select Bank Transfer

Choose **Bank Transfer** from the deposit options. The app will display the bank account details.

## Step 2 — Transfer from Your Bank

Use your internet banking or mobile banking app to transfer the amount to the provided account number. Include your 3 Patti Blue User ID in the transfer reference.

## Step 3 — Submit Transfer Receipt

After the transfer, upload your payment receipt inside the app. Your deposit will be credited within 1–3 hours after verification.

## Deposit Tips

- **Claim your first deposit bonus** — new players get up to PKR 5,000 on their first deposit. Check the [bonus guide](/3-patti-blue-bonus) before depositing.
- **Double-check the number** — always verify your JazzCash/EasyPaisa number before confirming.
- **Keep the OTP ready** — the OTP expires in 60 seconds, so confirm quickly.
- **Save your receipt** — always screenshot or save your deposit receipt for reference.

## Deposit Not Credited?

If your deposit is not showing after 30 minutes:

1. Check your JazzCash/EasyPaisa transaction history to confirm the payment went through.
2. Contact 3 Patti Blue live chat support with your transaction ID and amount.
3. Support will manually verify and credit the amount to your account.
