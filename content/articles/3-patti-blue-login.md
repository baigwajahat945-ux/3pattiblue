## How to Log In to 3 Patti Blue

Logging into your 3 Patti Blue account takes less than 30 seconds. Follow these steps:

## Step 1 — Open the App

Launch 3 Patti Blue on your Android phone. If you haven't installed it yet, [download the APK first](/).

## Step 2 — Tap "Log In"

On the home screen, tap the **Log In** button. Do not tap Sign Up if you already have an account.

## Step 3 — Enter Your Credentials

Enter your registered **phone number** and **password**. Then tap the **Login** button.

## Step 4 — You Are In!

You will be taken directly to the game lobby. Claim your daily bonus and start playing.

## Login Methods Available

3 Patti Blue supports multiple login methods:

- **Phone Number + Password** — most common method
- **OTP Login** — receive a one-time password via SMS
- **Guest Login** — play without an account (limited features, no withdrawals)

## Forgot Your Password?

If you forgot your password:

1. Tap **Forgot Password** on the login screen
2. Enter your registered phone number
3. You will receive an OTP via SMS
4. Enter the OTP and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

## Common Login Issues and Fixes

**Wrong password:** Double-check your password. Use the Forgot Password option to reset it.

**Account blocked:** Contact 3 Patti Blue support via live chat in the app.

**App not opening:** [Re-download the latest APK](/3-patti-blue-apk-download) and reinstall.

**SMS OTP not received:** Check your network. Request a new OTP after 60 seconds.

## Login on PC

If you play on PC via BlueStacks, the login process is identical. Enter your phone number and password in the app running inside BlueStacks. See our [PC guide](/3-patti-blue-for-pc) for setup instructions.
