## 3 Patti Blue Tournaments & Events 2026

One of the most exciting features of 3 Patti Blue is its regular tournament and event calendar. Whether you are a casual player or a serious competitor, tournaments give you the chance to win massive prize pools — sometimes reaching hundreds of thousands of PKR — just by playing the games you already love.

## Types of Tournaments on 3 Patti Blue

## Daily Tournaments

Daily tournaments run every 24 hours and are open to all players regardless of VIP level. Entry is either free or requires a small buy-in. Prize pools typically range from PKR 5,000 to PKR 20,000 distributed among the top 10–20 finishers.

- **Duration:** 24 hours
- **Entry:** Free or PKR 50–200 buy-in
- **Prize pool:** PKR 5,000–20,000
- **Games:** Teen Patti, Slots, Fishing

## Weekly Tournaments

Weekly tournaments run from Monday to Sunday with larger prize pools and more competition. These attract experienced players and offer significantly better rewards.

- **Duration:** 7 days
- **Entry:** PKR 100–500 buy-in
- **Prize pool:** PKR 50,000–200,000
- **Games:** Teen Patti Classic, Aviator, Rummy

## Monthly Mega Events

Once per month, 3 Patti Blue hosts a Mega Event — the biggest tournament on the calendar. These events have the largest prize pools and special bonus multipliers for all participants.

- **Duration:** Full month
- **Entry:** Free for VIP Silver and above
- **Prize pool:** PKR 500,000+
- **Special perks:** 2x VIP points, exclusive badge, leaderboard prizes

## Special Holiday Events

During Pakistani holidays, 3 Patti Blue runs limited-time special events with unique themes and boosted rewards:

- **Eid Special Tournament** — double prize pool, free entry for all players
- **Independence Day Challenge** — Pakistan-themed game rooms, PKR 1,000,000 prize pool
- **New Year Mega Spin** — spin-to-win events with guaranteed prizes
- **Ramadan Nights** — special late-night sessions with bonus multipliers

## How to Join a Tournament

## Step 1 — Open 3 Patti Blue

[Download and install](/) the app if you haven't already, then [log in to your account](/3-patti-blue-login).

## Step 2 — Go to the Tournament Section

Tap the **Tournament** or **Events** tab at the bottom of the home screen. You will see all currently active and upcoming tournaments.

## Step 3 — Choose Your Tournament

Browse the list and select a tournament that matches your budget and preferred game. Read the rules, entry fee, and prize distribution before joining.

## Step 4 — Pay the Entry Fee (if required)

Free tournaments have no cost. For paid tournaments, the entry fee is deducted from your wallet balance. Make sure your [wallet is funded](/3-patti-blue-deposit) before joining.

## Step 5 — Play and Climb the Leaderboard

Once registered, play the specified game during the tournament window. Your score or chip count determines your position on the leaderboard. The higher you rank, the bigger your share of the prize pool.

## Step 6 — Collect Your Winnings

Tournament prizes are automatically credited to your account at the end of the event. Winnings can be [withdrawn via JazzCash or EasyPaisa](/3-patti-blue-withdraw).

## Tips to Win 3 Patti Blue Tournaments

- **Enter early** — the leaderboard resets at the start, giving all players an equal chance
- **Play during off-peak hours** — competition is lower in early morning sessions
- **Use your bonus balance** — if you have bonus chips, use them during tournaments to preserve your real money balance
- **Focus on one game** — mastering one game gives better results than spreading across multiple games
- **Check VIP multipliers** — higher [VIP levels](/blog/3-patti-blue-vip-program) earn more points per wager during tournaments
