## How to Withdraw from 3 Patti Blue

Getting your winnings out of 3 Patti Blue is quick and easy. Most withdrawals are processed within 5–30 minutes directly to your mobile wallet.

## Withdrawal Methods

| Method | Min Amount | Max Amount | Processing Time |
|--------|-----------|------------|-----------------|
| EasyPaisa | PKR 200 | PKR 50,000 | 5–15 minutes |
| JazzCash | PKR 200 | PKR 50,000 | 5–15 minutes |
| Bank Transfer | PKR 500 | PKR 100,000 | 1–24 hours |

## Step-by-Step Withdrawal Guide

## Step 1 — Open the App

Launch 3 Patti Blue and make sure you are [logged in](/3-patti-blue-login).

## Step 2 — Go to Wallet

Tap the **Wallet** or **Cashier** icon at the bottom of the screen.

## Step 3 — Tap Withdraw

Select **Withdraw** from the options. You will see the withdrawal form.

## Step 4 — Choose Your Method

Select **JazzCash** or **EasyPaisa** (or Bank Transfer for larger amounts).

## Step 5 — Enter Account Details

Type your JazzCash or EasyPaisa mobile number exactly. Double-check it — wrong numbers cannot be reversed.

## Step 6 — Enter Amount

Enter the amount you want to withdraw. The minimum is **PKR 200**.

## Step 7 — Confirm

Tap **Confirm** or **Submit**. You may be asked to enter your withdrawal PIN or OTP for security.

## Step 8 — Receive Your Money

The money will arrive in your JazzCash or EasyPaisa account within 5–30 minutes.

## Important Rules Before Withdrawing

- Your account must be **verified** (phone number confirmed)
- If you claimed a bonus, you must meet the **wagering requirement** first
- Maximum 3 withdrawals per day
- Your withdrawal name must match your JazzCash/EasyPaisa registered name

## Withdrawal Not Processing? Common Fixes

**Pending for more than 1 hour:** Contact 3 Patti Blue live chat support with your withdrawal ID.

**Withdrawal rejected:** Check that your account is fully verified and bonus wagering is complete.

**Wrong account number:** Contact support immediately with your transaction details.
